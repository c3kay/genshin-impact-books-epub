# Foreword {epub:type=foreword}

The goal of this project is to make the ingame books of Genshin Impact available for offline
reading with e-readers.

This collection combines all books as individual chapters. The order of the books/chapters
in this collection follows the order of regions visited during the main quest line,
i.e. starting with Mondstadt and ending with scattered books all over Teyvat.
However, the books of a region don't have a reading order and are sorted alphabetically.

Texts have been taken from the Genshin Impact Wiki (<https://genshin-impact.fandom.com/wiki/Books>).
A few quest-item books have not been included in this collection, due to very short or (imo.)
irrelevant content.

New versions and updates of this collection will be available on GitHub:
<https://github.com/c3kay/genshin-impact-books-epub>
